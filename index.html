What is Google Chrome Dino Game?

The Google Chrome Dino Game is a fun and challenging game that appears when you're disconnected from the internet while using Google Chrome. The game features a cute dinosaur character that you control to jump over obstacles like cacti and avoid running into birds, all while keeping an eye on your score!

How to Play Google Chrome Dino Game

Playing the Google Chrome Dino Game is easy! Simply press the spacebar to jump and avoid obstacles. Keep playing to improve your score, and see how long you can last without crashing!
